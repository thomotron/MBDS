Button
If the button is red and says "Hold", press and immediately release the button.
If the button says "Reset", press and immediately release the button.
Otherwise, press and hold the button until the indicator lights up, then release the button and refer to "Coloured Indicators"

Coloured Indicators
If the indicator is orange, press and hold the button until the timer has a three in any position.
If the indicator is purple, press and hold the button until the timer has a six in any position.
If the indicator is blue, press and immediately release the button.
If the indicator is any other colour, press and hold the button until the timer has a one in any position.
